Ana, Cassel and Jonathan's Pig Dice

Rules of the Game:

  • Players roll two six-side dice.
  • Unless either of the dice roll a one, the sum of the two dice is added to the total score.
  • If a single one is rolled, the player that rolled the die loses that turn's score and ends their turn.
  • However, if two ones are rolled, the turn ends, but adds the score (2) to their total score.
  • First player to reach a certain set number wins.
